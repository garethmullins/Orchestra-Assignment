With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactor roll for twelve months. This roll will be published in each concert prgram and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also receive two complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Lunch function. Please complete the details below or fill in this form.

Donations are tax deductable. For more information contact Simon McConnell 0418776961, mcconnellgray@optusnet.com.au. Your support of the Orchestra will be greatly appreciated.

Sponsor or Donate to the Orchestra

The North Queensland Ensembles Inc is a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organisations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. Donations of $2.00 or more are tax deductible. Help us continue to provide quality entertainment for the Townsville community by supporting the orchestra. Contact us today.
