With a philanthropic gift of $200 or more (we leave that up to you) you can become an Orchestra Benefactor and be placed on our Benefactors Roll for twelve months. This Roll will be published in each concert program and on our website for twelve months. As a benefactor you will also receive two complimentary tickets to our next concert, be placed on the mailing list for our newsletter and will be a guest at our annual Season's Lunch function.
