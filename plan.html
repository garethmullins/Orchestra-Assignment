Goals

The main goals of the site are to increase awareness of the orchestra in the wider community and to attract people to concerts; musicians to join the orchestra; benefactors and sponsors to donate funds.

Redesigning this website to transform the initial visual appearance of the Barrier Reef Orchestra, to one more fitting of the current standing the orchestra has in the musical community

Success Evaluation

Success of the site can be evaluated by an increase in awareness of the orchestra and to attract people to concerts. This will be measured according to several factors as outlined below

  • Increased attendance at concerts
  • Increase in submission of contact forms from prospective supporters via new website
  • Increase in submission of contact forms from prospective members via new website
  • Increased ticket sales through the website.

    • Target Audience

    The website should appeal to the wider community but have a focus on attracting a younger audience
