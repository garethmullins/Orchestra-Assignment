Fast becoming one of North Queensland’s cultural jewels the Barrier Reef Orchestra this year launches its 16th year of performing orchestral music throughout North Queensland. A community orchestra consisting of skilled musicians of all ages and professions from as far afield as Cairns and Mackay looking to partake in an orchestra.
